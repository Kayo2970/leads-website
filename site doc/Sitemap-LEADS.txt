4. URL Rules
All developers and content editors working on the LEADS site must follow these rules when creating or editing any page URL:

	•	Always lowercase: /about-us not /About-Us
	•	Use hyphens, not underscores: /join-us not /join_us
	•	Keyword-first: /events/bharat-lead-summit not /events/summit/bls
	•	No trailing slash: /about not /about/
	•	No dates in permanent page URLs — dates only in blog/news slugs (if added later)
	•	Maximum 3 levels deep: /events/bharat-lead-summit is the deepest allowed in v1
	•	No special characters, spaces, or capital letters in any URL
